app overview

The easy-to-use ProNav Angler Mobile App (found on Google Play or iOS App Store) turns your phone or tablet into a powerful mobile chartplotter that can be used both on an off the water to plan your fishing and send GPS commands to your trolling motor for precise, automatic navigation. Quickly connect your smartphone or tablet to the Angler on or off the grid; the ProNav Angler does not require cell service or a data connection to function.

Control a full suite of navigational functions directly from your device without the need for a costly chartplotting sonar unit. The use of satellite imagery and optional Navionics lake maps allows you to create and manage navigation waypoints directly on your mobile device screen and instantly send navigation commands to your trolling motor via a robust Bluetooth connection. Another great benefit of being connected is that all of your data can be automatically backed up in the secure ProNav Cloud, synced across multiple devices, and shared with your trusted fishing partners.

The home screen of the ProNav Mobile App is laid out similar to a remote control and can be used to send basic manual commands to your trolling motor in addition to GPS commands including anchor at your current location, lock your motor heading, and cruise control.
