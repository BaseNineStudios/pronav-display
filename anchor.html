GPS Anchor

Regardless of wind, waves, or current, the ProNav™ Angler’s Anchor can hold your boat in a precise position at the press of a button. Anchor points are easy to select from the ProNav Angler mobile app Live Map View, and the Angler’s Go-To-Anchor feature can guide you to a distant anchor point from any location. Unlimited anchor points can be saved and shared with friends using the ProNav Cloud!

  • Rope-less anchor maintains your boat’s position in wind, waves, and current
  • Set anchor point at a current position or choose an anchor point from Live Map View
  • Go-to-Anchor brings you directly to an anchor point from your current location, allowing you to get organized or rigged up on your way to your next spot
  • Save your anchor point so you never lose a good spot again
  • Save & Share UNLIMITED anchor points in the ProNav Cloud
