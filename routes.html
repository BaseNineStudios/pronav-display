Easy to Use Routes

The ProNav™ Angler makes it easier than ever to create and modify unique routes on your favorite bodies of water. Unlimited routes can be created on or off the water from your mobile device. Select points to create routes in Live Map View using detailed satellite imagery or Navionics lake maps (requires Navionics subscription). The ProNav Angler Routes feature will keep your boat on course so that you can focus on fishing.

  • Create and modify routes from your mobile device
  • Use Live Map View to select points to create route
  • Navionics Map Integration
  • Create and store UNLIMITED routes
  • Record, Retrace, and share routes with ProNav friends

Create Routes On and Off Water

With the ProNav Mobile Application, you can create and modify routes from your mobile device before you even hit the water. Map data can be “cached” (saved) to your device over Wi-Fi while at home for access in remote, “off-the-grid” locations where mobile data is not available. When you are on the water, the Angler uses a Bluetooth connection between your mobile device and the Angler’s high-accuracy GPS and heading sensors to follow the route you created.
