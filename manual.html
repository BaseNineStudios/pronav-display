Manual Control

The ProNav™ Angler™ connects to your bow mounted trolling motor and is controlled from your smartphone or tablet. Adjust the speed and heading of your motor through the ProNav Angler mobile app. Your existing foot pedal can still be used for hands free adjustments. View your heading and progress on the live map view.

  • Set a compass heading lock
  • Maintain a speed using cruise control
  • Manually adjust motor thrust and steering direction
  • Seamless integration with your existing foot pedal
