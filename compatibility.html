Compatibility

Motors with iPilot and Autopilot

If your motor is equipped with an iPilot or Minn Kota Autopilot:

  • Removal of existing i-Pilot or autopilot accessories is required
  • Removal of existing i-Pilot or autopilot accessories (if factory installed) may require replacement of motor control board
